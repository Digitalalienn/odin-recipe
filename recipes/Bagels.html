Homemade Bagels

Description

Easy, warm soft bagels! These bagels are perfect to make at home for any occuasion

Ingredients

    For the dough

  • 4 cups bread flour, plus more kneading
  • 1 tablespoon packed light brown sugar
  • 1 tablepoons active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • Cooking Spray

    For the wath bath

  • 8 cups (2 quarts) water
  • 3 tablespoons packed light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspon baking soda
  • 1 teaspon kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup topping,such as everything bagel seasing, poppy seeds, or sesame seeds (optional)
